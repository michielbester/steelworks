Key Risks
!
Production cost ceiling. COGS must stay below R10/can to maintain the 40% minimum margin. Any supplier price increase directly compresses profitability.
!
Daily delivery complexity. Golf clubs prefer frequent top-ups. Own delivery at scale is expensive — a distribution partner will likely be required from day one.
!
Recipe loyalty risk. Each club has its own Steelworks recipe. Changing an established recipe risks alienating 10-year loyalists and damaging a club's guest experience.
!
Cannibalization, not new demand. A canned Steelworks may replace the house-made version rather than adding a net new sale — shifting margin risk to the club.
